  description: |
    The snakes can be replaced with eaters to form a slightly smaller
    version, as shown in the p46 MWSS gun in [gliderless](/patterns/gliderless)
    The period of this new oscillator is the same as the old
    [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle), and so this is able to expand the known p46
    [technology](/patterns/technology).  For example, a p46 glider gun can be made from a
    Tanner's p46 and just one of the [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle)s.
    Acting on their own, two copies of Tanner's p46 placed at right
    angles to each other with their sparks interacting can produce two
    different p46 glider guns and a gliderless p46 MWSS gun.  See
    [p46 gun](/patterns/p46-gun) and [gliderless](/patterns/gliderless) for two of these. These are the first p46
    guns found which do not use a twin bees shuttle at all.
