description: |
  The first known [gun](/patterns/gun), and indeed the first known
  finite pattern displaying [infinite growth](/patterns/infinite-growth), found by Bill Gosper in
  November 1970.  This period 30 gun remains the smallest known gun in
  terms of its bounding box, though some variants of the p120
  [Simkin glider gun](/patterns/simkin-glider-gun) have a lower population. Gosper later constructed
  several other guns, such as [new gun](/patterns/new-gun) and the p144 gun shown under
  [factory](/patterns/factory).  See also [p30 gun](/patterns/p30-gun).
