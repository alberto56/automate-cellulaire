description: |
  (p120)  A [Herschel](/patterns/herschel)-based glider gun discovered by
  Michael Simkin in April 2015.  It consists of a Herschel running
  through two [B60](/patterns/b60) conduits. In terms of its 36-cell minimum
  population, it is one of the smallest known guns, sharing the record
  with the [Gosper glider gun](/patterns/gosper-glider-gun). In the double-barreled form, as well as
  the [pseudo](/patterns/pseudo)-period, [snake](/patterns/snake)-stabilized form shown below, it is the
  absolute record holder.
