description: |
  A [composite conduit](/patterns/composite-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Dave Buckingham in May 1997.  It
  is made up of two [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit)s,  HR143B + [BFx59H](/patterns/bfx59h).  After
  202 ticks, it produces an inverted [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) turned 90 degrees
  clockwise at (7, 32) relative to the input.  Its [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) is
  201 ticks.  A [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern below marks the output
  location.
