description: |
  A small periodic variant of a stable two-glider-to-Herschel
  component found by Paul Callahan in November 1998  and used in the
  [Callahan G-to-H](/patterns/callahan-g-to-h), [Silver reflector](/patterns/silver-reflector) and [Silver G-to-H](/patterns/silver-g-to-h).  The
  minimum [repeat time](/patterns/repeat-time) is 192 ticks, though some lower periods such as
  96 are possible via [overclocking](/patterns/overclocking).  Here a [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) marks
  the output signal location.
