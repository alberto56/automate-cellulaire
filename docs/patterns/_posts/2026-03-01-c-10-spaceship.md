description: |
  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) travelling at one tenth of the
  [speed of light](/patterns/speed-of-light).  The first such spaceship to be discovered was the
  orthogonally travelling [copperhead](/patterns/copperhead), found by 'zdr' on 5 March 2016.
  Simon Ekstrom found the related [fireship](/patterns/fireship) two weeks later.  A
  [Caterloopillar](/patterns/caterloopillar) can theoretically be configured to move at c/10, but
  there are technical difficulties with speeds of the form 4n+2, and as
  of June 2018 this has not been done in practice.
