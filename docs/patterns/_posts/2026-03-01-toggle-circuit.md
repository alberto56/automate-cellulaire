description: |
  Any signal-processing [circuit](/patterns/circuit) that switches back and
  forth between two possible states or outputs.  An early example is
  the [boat-bit](/patterns/boat-bit).  More recent discoveries include the [semi-Snark](/patterns/semi-snark)s,
  which alternate between reflecting and absorbing input [glider](/patterns/glider)s.
  The following B-to-G [converter](/patterns/converter) sends alternate glider outputs in
  opposite directions.
