description: |
  A stable or oscillating linearly repeating pattern that can be
  made to [burn](/patterns/burn) at one end.  See [fuse](/patterns/fuse).  Wicks are often fairly
  dense, with repeating units directly connected or at least adjacent
  to each other, as in the beehive [lightspeed wire](/patterns/lightspeed-wire) for example.
  However, sparse wicks such as the blocks in the
  [31c/240 Herschel-pair climber](/patterns/31c/240-herschel-pair-climber) are known, and arbitrarily sparse
  wicks can be constructed.
