  A 25-cell quadratic growth pattern found by
  Michael Simkin in October 2014, with a bounding box of 21372x172. It
  was the smallest-population quadratic growth pattern for two days,
  until the discovery of [24-cell quadratic growth](/patterns/24-cell-quadratic-growth). It superseded
  [wedge](/patterns/wedge), which had held the record for eight years.  See
  [switch-engine ping-pong](/patterns/switch-engine-ping-pong) for the lowest-population
  [superlinear growth](/patterns/superlinear-growth) pattern as of July 2018, along with a list of
  the record-holders.
