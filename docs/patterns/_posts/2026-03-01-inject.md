description: |
  A reaction in which a hole in a regular spaceship stream is
  filled partially or fully by adding a new spaceship of the same type
  without affecting the existing spaceships in the stream.  Depending
  on the period of the stream, different mechanisms can be used.  For
  adding a spaceship to an existing multi-lane [convoy](/patterns/convoy), see
  [inserter](/patterns/inserter).
  For large period glider streams, simple reactions such as
  [LWSS-LWSS bounce](/patterns/lwss-lwss-bounce) and [LWSS-glider bounce](/patterns/lwss-glider-bounce) suffice.  If [Herschel](/patterns/herschel)
  technology is used, a large number of [edge shooter](/patterns/edge-shooter)s and
  [transparent](/patterns/transparent) conduits are known.  Simple examples include the [NW31](/patterns/nw31)
  [Herschel-to-glider](/patterns/herschel-to-glider) [converter](/patterns/converter) and the [Fx119 inserter](/patterns/fx119-inserter).
  Shown below is an injector found by Dave Buckingham that can fill a
  hole in a p15 glider stream.
