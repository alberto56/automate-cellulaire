  A [true](/patterns/true) period 256 four-barrelled [glider gun](/patterns/glider-gun) found by
  Dave Buckingham in September 1995.  It uses four [R64](/patterns/r64) [conduit](/patterns/conduit)s to
  make the second smallest known [Herschel loop](/patterns/herschel-loop) (after the
  [Simkin glider gun](/patterns/simkin-glider-gun)).  The p256 gun was an early "teaser" from Dave
  Buckingham before he released his full [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) [technology](/patterns/technology).
