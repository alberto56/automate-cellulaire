description: |
  A term used for [turner](/patterns/turner)s and [splitter](/patterns/splitter)s, specifying that
  the reaction in question is not repeatable as it would be in a
  [reflector](/patterns/reflector) or [fanout](/patterns/fanout) device.  Instead, the [constellation](/patterns/constellation) is used
  up, usually in a [clean](/patterns/clean) reaction, but the much more common [dirty](/patterns/dirty)
  turners and splitters are also very useful in some situations.
