  Dean Hickerson's term for an [eater](/patterns/eater) which remains intact
  throughout the eating process.  The [snake](/patterns/snake) in Dave Buckingham's
  59-step B-to-Herschel conduit (shown under [conduit](/patterns/conduit)) is an example.
  Other still lifes that sometimes act as rocks include the [tub](/patterns/tub), the
  [hook with tail](/patterns/hook-with-tail), the [eater1](/patterns/eater1) (eating with its tail) and the [hat](/patterns/hat)
  (in Heinrich Koenig's stabilization of the [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle)).
