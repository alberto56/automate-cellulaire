description: |
  A [lane](/patterns/lane) or [signal](/patterns/signal) path used in construction circuitry.
  Until the invention of [single-channel](/patterns/single-channel) [construction arm](/patterns/construction-arm)s, signals
  in a channel would usually be [synchronized](/patterns/synchronized) with one or more
  coordinated signals on other paths, as in the [Gemini](/patterns/gemini), which used
  twelve channels to run three construction arms simultaneously, or the
  10hd [Demonoid](/patterns/demonoid) which needed only two channels.  See also [Geminoid](/patterns/geminoid).
