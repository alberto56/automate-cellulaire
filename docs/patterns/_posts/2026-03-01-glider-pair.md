description: |
  Two gliders travelling in the same direction with a
  specific spacetime offset.  In a [transceiver](/patterns/transceiver) the preferred term is
  [tandem glider](/patterns/tandem-glider).  For several years, glider pairs on [lane](/patterns/lane)s
  separated by 9 or 10 [half diagonal](/patterns/half-diagonal)s were the standard building
  blocks in [Geminoid](/patterns/geminoid) [construction arm](/patterns/construction-arm) [recipe](/patterns/recipe)s.  In more recent
  0hd and [single-channel](/patterns/single-channel) construction toolkits, all gliders share the
  same lane, but glider pairs and [singleton](/patterns/singleton)s are still important
  concepts.
