description: |
  To hammer a [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs), [MWSS](/patterns/mwSs) or [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs) is to smash things into
  the rear end of it in order to transform it into a different type of
  [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship).  A hammer is the object used to do the hammering. In the
  following example by Dieter Leithner an LWSS is hammered by two more
  LWSS to make it into an MWSS.
