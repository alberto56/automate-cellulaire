  Any [sawtooth](/patterns/sawtooth) pattern with a quadratic envelope,
  or specifically a pattern assembled by Martin Grant in May 2015,
  consisting of two [caber tosser](/patterns/caber-tosser)s with period multipliers for timing
  which activate and deactivate two toggleable rake guns (see
  [toggleable gun](/patterns/toggleable-gun)).  The gliders emitted by those rakes annihilate on
  the diagonal while the rakes are eaten by 2c/5 ships. All the rakes
  and gliders are destroyed before the next cycle.  See also
  [Osqrtlogt](/patterns/osqrtlogt).
