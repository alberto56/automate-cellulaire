description: |
  A pattern composed of subunits that can be easily constructed
  in any orientation, usually with a [slow salvo](/patterns/slow-salvo). Generally this means
  that the pattern is a [constellation](/patterns/constellation) of Spartan still lifes:
  [block](/patterns/block), [tub](/patterns/tub), [boat](/patterns/boat), [hive](/patterns/hive), [ship](/patterns/ship), [loaf](/patterns/loaf), [eater1](/patterns/eater1), or [pond](/patterns/pond).
  Other small objects may sometimes be counted as Spartan, including
  period-2 oscillators - mainly [blinker](/patterns/blinker)s, but also [beacon](/patterns/beacon)s or
  [toad](/patterns/toad)s, which may occur as [intermediate target](/patterns/intermediate-target)s in slow salvo
  [recipe](/patterns/recipe)s.  Most [self-constructing](/patterns/self-constructing) patterns are Spartan or mostly
  Spartan, to simplify the process of self-construction.
