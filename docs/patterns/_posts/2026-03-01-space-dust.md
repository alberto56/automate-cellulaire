  A part of a [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) or [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) which looks like a
  random mix of ON and OFF cells.  It is usually very difficult to find
  a [glider synthesis](/patterns/glider-synthesis) for an object that consists wholly or partly of
  space dust.  As examples, the [295P5H1V1](/patterns/295p5h1v1), [fly](/patterns/fly), and [seal](/patterns/seal)
  spaceships contain large amounts of space dust.
