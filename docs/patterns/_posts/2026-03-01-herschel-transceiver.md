  An adjustable [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit) made up of a
  [Herschel transmitter](/patterns/herschel-transmitter) and a [Herschel receiver](/patterns/herschel-receiver).  The intermediate
  stage consists of a [tandem glider](/patterns/tandem-glider) - two [glider](/patterns/glider)s on parallel
  [lane](/patterns/lane)s - so that the transmitter and receiver can be separated by
  any required distance. The conduit may be [stable](/patterns/stable), or may contain
  low-period [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator)s.
