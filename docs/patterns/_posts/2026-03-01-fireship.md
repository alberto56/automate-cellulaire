description: |
  (c/10 orthogonally, p10)  A variant of the [copperhead](/patterns/copperhead) with
  a trailing component that emits several large [spark](/patterns/spark)s, discovered by
  Simon Ekstrom on 20 March 2016.  The interaction between the
  copperhead and the additional component is minimal enough that the
  extension technically fits the definition of a [tagalong](/patterns/tagalong).  However,
  the extension slightly modifies two of the [phase](/patterns/phase)s of the spaceship,
  starting two ticks after the phase shown below, so it's also valid to
  classify the fireship as a distinct spaceship.
