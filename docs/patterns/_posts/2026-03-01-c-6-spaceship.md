  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) travelling at one sixth of the
  [speed of light](/patterns/speed-of-light).  The first such spaceship to be discovered was the
  [dragon](/patterns/dragon), found by Paul Tooke in April 2000.  The first diagonally
  moving example was the [seal](/patterns/seal), found by Nicolay Beluchenko in
  September 2005.  Another orthogonal c/6 spaceship, found by Paul
  Tooke in March 2006, is shown below.  For the smallest known c/6
  spaceship see [56P6H1V0](/patterns/56p6h1v0).
