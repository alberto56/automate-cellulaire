description: |
  (c/3 orthogonally, p3)  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) found by Dean Hickerson
  in August 1989 that produces a [domino](/patterns/domino) [spark](/patterns/spark) at the back.
  Hickerson used this spark to convert an approaching [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs) into a
  [loaf](/patterns/loaf), as part of the first [sawtooth](/patterns/sawtooth).  (Also see [tractor beam](/patterns/tractor-beam)).
  The shape of the back end of the turtle is distinctive.  Very similar
  but wider back ends have been found in other c/3 ships to produce
  period 9 and 15 [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s.
