description: |
  A glider gun which has true-period 46.  The first one found
  was the [new gun](/patterns/new-gun) by Bill Gosper in 1971.  Prior to the discovery of
  [Tanner's p46](/patterns/tanner-s-p46) in October 2017, all known p46 guns were made from two
  or more [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle)s that interact (e.g., see
  [twin bees shuttle pair](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle-pair)). See [edge shooter](/patterns/edge-shooter) and [double-barrelled](/patterns/double-barrelled)
  for two more of these.
  On 21 October 2017 Heinrich Koenig found a glider gun using two
  copies of [Tanner's p46](/patterns/tanner-s-p46) placed at right angles to each other.  This
  is the first p46 gun found which makes no use of the
  [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle).
