description: |
  An online database of objects in Conway's Game of Life and
  similar cellular automata, set up by Adam P. Goucher in 2015 at
  [http://catagolue.appspot.com](http://catagolue.appspot.com). It gathers data from a distributed
  search of random initial configurations and records the eventual
  decay products.  Within a year of operation it had completed a
  [census](/patterns/census) of the [ash](/patterns/ash) objects from over two trillion asymmetric 16x16
  [soup](/patterns/soup)s.  As of June 2018, well over two hundred trillion ash objects
  have been counted, from over a trillion asymmetric soups.
  It is often possible to use Catagolue search results find
  equivalent [glider synthesis](/patterns/glider-synthesis) recipes for selected parts of
  long-running active reactions.  These random [soup](/patterns/soup) searches have
  made it possible to find efficient construction methods for thousands
  of increasingly rare [still life](/patterns/still-life)s and [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator)s, and the
  occasional [puffer](/patterns/puffer) or [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship).  In many of these cases a
  [glider synthesis](/patterns/glider-synthesis) was previously very difficult or unknown.
