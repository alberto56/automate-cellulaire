description: |
  One of the three [elementary](/patterns/elementary) conduits used in the composite
  [Fx176](/patterns/fx176) [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit).  It converts an input [pi-heptomino](/patterns/pi-heptomino) into
  an output [wing](/patterns/wing) in 35 ticks.  In November 2017, Aidan F. Pierce
  discovered the compact PF35W variant below, which improved the repeat
  time of the Fx176 to 73 ticks and allowed [glider](/patterns/glider)s from following
  [dependent conduit](/patterns/dependent-conduit)s to escape freely.
