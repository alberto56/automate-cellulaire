description: |
  The type of [universal constructor](/patterns/universal-constructor) exemplified by the
  original [Gemini](/patterns/gemini) spaceship, where two independently programmed
  [construction arm](/patterns/construction-arm)s sent gliders in pairs on 90-degree paths to
  collide with each other at the construction site.  Construction
  recipes for two-arm constructors are much more efficient in general,
  but they require many more [circuit](/patterns/circuit)s and multiple independent data
  streams, which both tend to increase the complexity of
  [self-constructing](/patterns/self-constructing) circuitry.  Compare [single-arm](/patterns/single-arm).
