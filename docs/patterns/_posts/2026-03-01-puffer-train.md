  The full name for a [puffer](/patterns/puffer), coined by Conway before

  the classic puffer train found by Bill Gosper and shown below. This
  is very [dirty](/patterns/dirty), and the tail does not stabilize until generation
  5533.  It consists of a [B-heptomino](/patterns/b-heptomino) (shown here one generation
  before the standard form) escorted by two [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs).  (This was the
  second known puffer.  The first is shown under [puffer](/patterns/puffer).)
