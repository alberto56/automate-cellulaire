description: |
  A finite pattern which repeatedly creates copies of
  itself.  Such objects are known to exist (see
  [universal constructor](/patterns/universal-constructor)), but no concrete example is known.  The
  [linear propagator](/patterns/linear-propagator) may be considered to be the first example of a
  replicator built in Life, but this is debatable as each of its copies
  replicates itself only once, allowing no possibility of
  [superlinear growth](/patterns/superlinear-growth).
