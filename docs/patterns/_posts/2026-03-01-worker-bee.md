description: |
  (p9)  Found by Dave Buckingham in 1972.  Unlike the similar
  [snacker](/patterns/snacker) this produces no [spark](/patterns/spark)s, and so is not very important.
  Like the snacker, the worker bee is [extensible](/patterns/extensible).  It is, in fact, a
  finite version of the infinite oscillator which consists of six ON
  cells and two OFF cells alternating along a line.  Note that Dean
  Hickerson's new snacker ends also work here.
