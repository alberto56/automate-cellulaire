description: |
  The smallest number of generations it takes for an
  [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) or [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) to reappear in its original form.  The
  term can also be used for a [puffer](/patterns/puffer), [wick](/patterns/wick), [fuse](/patterns/fuse), [superstring](/patterns/superstring),
  stream of [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s, [factory](/patterns/factory) or [gun](/patterns/gun).  In the last case there
  is a distinction between [true](/patterns/true) period and [pseudo](/patterns/pseudo) period.  There is
  also a somewhat different concept of period for [wicktrailer](/patterns/wicktrailer)s.
