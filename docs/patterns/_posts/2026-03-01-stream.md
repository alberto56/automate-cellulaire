description: |
  A line of identical objects (usually [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s), each of
  which is moving in a direction parallel to the line, generally on the
  same [lane](/patterns/lane).  In many uses the stream is periodic.  For example, the
  [new gun](/patterns/new-gun) produces a period 46 [glider](/patterns/glider) stream.  The stream produced
  by a [pseudo-random glider generator](/patterns/pseudo-random-glider-generator) can have a very high period.
  Compare with [wave](/patterns/wave).  See also [single-channel](/patterns/single-channel) for a common use of
  non-periodic [glider](/patterns/glider) streams.
