description: |
  (c/2 orthogonally, p4)  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) found by Hartmut
  Holzwart in July 1992.  (The name is due to Bill Gosper.)  It
  consists of a [pre-beehive](/patterns/pre-beehive) escorted by four [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs).  In fact any
  LWSS can be replaced by a [MWSS](/patterns/mwSs) or an [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs), so that there are 45
  different single-hive hivenudgers.
