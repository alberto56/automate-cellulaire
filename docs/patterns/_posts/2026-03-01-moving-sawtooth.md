description: |
  A [sawtooth](/patterns/sawtooth) such that no cell is ON for more than a
  finite number generations.  David Bell constructed the first pattern
  of this type, with a c/2 front end and a c/3 back end.  The front end
  is a [blinker puffer](/patterns/blinker-puffer).  The back end ignites the [blinker fuse](/patterns/blinker-fuse).
  The smallest currently known moving sawtooth was constructed in
  April 2011 by a conwaylife.com forum user with the handle
  'cloudy197'.  The c/2 front end is a [bi-block puffer](/patterns/bi-block-puffer).  The 2c/5
  back end ignites the [bi-block fuse](/patterns/bi-block-fuse).
