description: |
  An adjustable mechanism in a [universal constructor](/patterns/universal-constructor)
  that allows new objects to be constructed in any chosen location that
  the arm can reach.  A construction arm generally consists of a
  [shoulder](/patterns/shoulder) containing fixed guns or edge shooters, a movable
  [construction elbow](/patterns/construction-elbow) that slides forward and backward along the
  [construction lane](/patterns/construction-lane)(s), and in the case of [single-arm](/patterns/single-arm) universal
  constructors, a [hand](/patterns/hand) target object at the construction site that
  can be progressively modified by a [slow salvo](/patterns/slow-salvo) to produce each
  desired object.
