description: |
  An [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Paul Callahan in November 1998.
  After 125 ticks, it produces an inverted [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) rotated 180
  degrees at (-9, -17) relative to the input.  Its [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) is
  166 ticks.  A [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern below marks the output
  location.
