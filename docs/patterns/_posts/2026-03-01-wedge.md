  held the record for the smallest-population quadratic growth pattern
  for eight years, until it was surpassed by
  [25-cell quadratic growth](/patterns/25-cell-quadratic-growth).  See [switch-engine ping-pong](/patterns/switch-engine-ping-pong) for the
  lowest-population [superlinear growth](/patterns/superlinear-growth) pattern as of July 2018, along
  with a list of the record-holders.
