description: |
  A head-on collision between three [glider](/patterns/glider)s, producing a
  perpendicular output glider that can be used to construct closely
  spaced glider [salvo](/patterns/salvo)s, or to [inject](/patterns/inject) a glider into an existing
  [stream](/patterns/stream).  There are several workable [recipe](/patterns/recipe)s.  One of the more
  useful is the following, because the [tandem glider](/patterns/tandem-glider) can be generated
  by a small [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) [converter](/patterns/converter), [SW1T43](/patterns/sw1t43).
