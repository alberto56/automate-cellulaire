  A necessary component of a [slow salvo](/patterns/slow-salvo) recipe used by a
  [single-arm](/patterns/single-arm) [universal constructor](/patterns/universal-constructor).  A target usually consists of a
  single object, or sometimes a small [constellation](/patterns/constellation) of common still
  lifes and/or oscillators.  See [intermediate target](/patterns/intermediate-target).  If no [hand](/patterns/hand)
  target is available, a construction arm may be unable to construct
  anything, unless recipes are available to generate targets directly
  from the [elbow](/patterns/elbow).
