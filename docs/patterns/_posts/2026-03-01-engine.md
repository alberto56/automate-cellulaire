description: |
  The active portion of an object (usually a [puffer](/patterns/puffer) or [gun](/patterns/gun))
  which is considered to actually produce its output, and which
  generally permits no variation in how it works.  The other parts of
  the object are just there to support the engine.  For examples, see
  [puffer train](/patterns/puffer-train), [Schick engine](/patterns/schick-engine), [blinker puffer](/patterns/blinker-puffer), [frothing puffer](/patterns/frothing-puffer)
  and [line puffer](/patterns/line-puffer).
