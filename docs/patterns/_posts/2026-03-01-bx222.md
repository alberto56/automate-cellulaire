description: |
  A [composite conduit](/patterns/composite-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Paul Callahan in October 1998.  It
  is made up of three [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit)s, HF95P + PB68B + [BFx59H](/patterns/bfx59h).
  After 222 ticks, it produces a mirror-reflected [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) rotated
  180 degrees, at (6, -16) relative to the input.  Its [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time)
  is 271 ticks.  A [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern below marks the
  output location.
