description: |
  An [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Dave Buckingham in September 1996.
  After 119 ticks, it produces an inverted [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) at (20, 14)
  relative to the input.  Its recovery time is 231 ticks; this can be
  reduced somewhat by suppressing the output Herschel's glider, or by
  adding extra [catalyst](/patterns/catalyst)s to make the reaction settle more quickly. A
  [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern below marks the output location.
