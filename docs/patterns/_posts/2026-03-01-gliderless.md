description: |
  A [gun](/patterns/gun) is said to be gliderless if it does not use
  [glider](/patterns/glider)s.  The purist definition would insist that a glider does not
  appear anywhere, even incidentally.  For a long time the only known
  way to construct [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs), [MWSS](/patterns/mwSs) and [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs) guns involved gliders, and
  it was not until April 1996 that Dieter Leithner constructed the
  first gliderless gun (a p46 LWSS gun).
  In October 2017 Matthias Merzenich used two copies of
  [Tanner's p46](/patterns/tanner-s-p46) to create a p46 MWSS gun.  This is the smallest known
  gliderless gun, and also the smallest known MWSS gun.
