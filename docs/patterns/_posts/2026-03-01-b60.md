description: |
  A [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit) discovered by Michael Simkin in 2015 using his search program, [CatForce](/patterns/catforce).  It is one of two known [Blockic](/patterns/blockic) [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit)s.  After 60 ticks, it produces a Herschel rotated 180 degrees at (-6,-10) relative to the input.  It can most easily be connected to another B60 conduit, producing a closed loop, the [Simkin glider gun](/patterns/simkin-glider-gun).
