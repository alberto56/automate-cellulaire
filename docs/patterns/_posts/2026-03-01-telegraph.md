description: |
  A pattern created by Jason Summers in February 2003.  It
  transmits and receives information using a rare type of
  [reburnable fuse](/patterns/reburnable-fuse), a [lightspeed wire](/patterns/lightspeed-wire) made from a chain of beehives,
  at the rate of 1440 ticks per bit.  The rate of travel of signals
  through the entire [transceiver](/patterns/transceiver) device can be increased to any speed
  strictly less than the [speed of light](/patterns/speed-of-light) by increasing the length of
  the beehive chain appropriately.
  "Telegraph" may also refer to any device that sends and receives
  lightspeed signals; see also [p1 telegraph](/patterns/p1-telegraph),
  [high-bandwidth telegraph](/patterns/high-bandwidth-telegraph).
