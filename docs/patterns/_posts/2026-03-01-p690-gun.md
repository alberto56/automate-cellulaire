description: |
  A [true](/patterns/true) period 690 [glider](/patterns/glider) gun found by Noam Elkies in
  July 1996.  It is composed of a p30 [queen bee shuttle pair](/patterns/queen-bee-shuttle-pair) and a
  p46 [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle) whose sparks occasionally react with each
  other.  This is a very compact gun for such a high period and is used
  in many patterns requiring sparse glider streams.
