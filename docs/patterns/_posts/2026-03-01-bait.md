description: |
  An object in a [converter](/patterns/converter), usually a small [still life](/patterns/still-life), that
  is temporarily destroyed by an incoming [signal](/patterns/signal), but in such a way
  that a usable output signal is produced.  In general such a converter
  produces multiple output signals (or a signal [splitter](/patterns/splitter) is added)
  and one branch of the output is routed to a [factory](/patterns/factory) mechanism that
  rebuilds the bait object so that the converter can be re-used.
