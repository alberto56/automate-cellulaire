description: |
  A stationary [elbow](/patterns/elbow) in a [construction arm](/patterns/construction-arm) [toolkit](/patterns/toolkit)
  that allows a [recipe](/patterns/recipe) to turn a corner with no exponential increase
  in construction cost.  Compare [slow elbow](/patterns/slow-elbow).  It is theoretically
  possible to construct lossless elbows for early construction arms
  such as the one in the [10hd Demonoid](/patterns/10hd-demonoid), but these would currently
  have to be very large.
  The lossless elbow that has been used the most in practice is the
  [Snark](/patterns/snark), which can be constructed directly on a [single-channel](/patterns/single-channel)
  [construction lane](/patterns/construction-lane) using a [Snarkmaker](/patterns/snarkmaker) [recipe](/patterns/recipe).  Controlled
  demolition of a Snark is also possible, to remove a temporary elbow
  that is no longer needed, and leave a [hand](/patterns/hand) target in its place if
  necessary for further construction.
  A [Silver reflector](/patterns/silver-reflector) was used as a lossless elbow in the first
  [spiral growth](/patterns/spiral-growth) pattern, attached to a separate
  [universal constructor](/patterns/universal-constructor) component.
