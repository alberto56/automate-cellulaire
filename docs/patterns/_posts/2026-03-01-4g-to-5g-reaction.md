description: |
  A reaction involving 4 gliders which cleanly
  produces 5 gliders.  The one shown below was found by Dieter Leithner
  in July 1992.      The first two gliders collide to produce a [traffic light](/patterns/traffic-light) and
  glider.  The other two gliders react symmetrically with the evolving
  [traffic light](/patterns/traffic-light) to form four gliders.  A [glider gun](/patterns/glider-gun) can be built by
  using [reflector](/patterns/reflector)s to turn four of the output gliders so that they
  repeat the reaction.
