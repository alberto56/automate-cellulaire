description: |
  (p4)  Found by Achim Flammenkamp in 1988, but not widely known
  until Dean Hickerson rediscovered it (and named it) in August 1989.
  Compare with [jam](/patterns/jam).  In terms of its minimum [population](/patterns/population) of 12 it
  ties with [mazing](/patterns/mazing) as the smallest p4 [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator).  But in terms of
  its 6x6 [bounding box](/patterns/bounding-box) it wins outright.  In fact, of all oscillators
  that fit in a 6x7 box it is the only one with [period](/patterns/period) greater than
  2.
