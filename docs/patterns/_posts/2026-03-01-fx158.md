description: |
  An [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Dave Buckingham in July 1996.
  After 158 ticks, it produces an inverted [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) at (27, -5)
  relative to the input.  Its [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) is 176 ticks.  It is the
  only known small conduit that does not produce its output Herschel
  via the usual [Herschel great-grandparent](/patterns/herschel-great-grandparent), so it cannot be followed
  by a [dependent conduit](/patterns/dependent-conduit).  A [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern below
  marks the output location.
