description: |
  For an [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) or [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship), the average number of cells
  which change state in each generation.  For example, the heat of a
  [glider](/patterns/glider) is 4, because 2 cells are born and 2 die every generation.
  For a period n oscillator with an r-cell [rotor](/patterns/rotor) the heat is at
  least 2r/n and no more than r(1-(n mod 2)/n).  For n=2 and n=3 these
  bounds are equal.
