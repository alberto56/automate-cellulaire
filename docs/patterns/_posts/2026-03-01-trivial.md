description: |
  A trivial period-N oscillator is one in which every cell
  oscillates at some smaller factor of N.  See [omniperiodic](/patterns/omniperiodic).  For
  example, the joining of a period 3 and a period 4 [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) as
  shown below creates a single object which is a trivial oscillator of
  period 12.
