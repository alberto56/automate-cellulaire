  (2c/7 orthogonally, p7)  Found by David Eppstein in January
  2000.  In April 2000 Stephen Silver found a tagalong for a pair of
  weekenders.  At present, n weekenders pulling n-1 tagalongs
  constitute the only known [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s of this speed or period,
  except for variants of the [weekender distaff](/patterns/weekender-distaff) that suppress its
  output gliders.
