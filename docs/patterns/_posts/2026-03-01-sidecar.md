description: |
  A small [tagalong](/patterns/tagalong) for an [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs) that was found by Hartmut
  Holzwart in 1992.  The resulting [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) (shown below) has a
  [phase](/patterns/phase) with only 24 cells, making it in this respect the smallest
  known spaceship other than the [standard spaceship](/patterns/standard-spaceship)s and some trivial
  two-spaceship [flotilla](/patterns/flotilla)s derived from them.  Note also that an HWSS
  can support two sidecars at once.
