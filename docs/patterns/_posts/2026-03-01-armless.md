description: |
  A method of generating [slow salvo](/patterns/slow-salvo)s across a wide range of
  lanes without using a [construction arm](/patterns/construction-arm) with a movable [elbow](/patterns/elbow).
  Instead, streams of gliders on two fixed opposing [lane](/patterns/lane)s collide
  with each other to produce clean 90-degree output gliders.  Slowing
  down one of the streams by 8N ticks will move the output lanes of the
  gliders toward the source of that stream by N [full diagonal](/patterns/full-diagonal)s.  This
  construction method was used to create the supporting slow salvos in
  the [half-baked knightship](/patterns/half-baked-knightship)s, and also in the [Parallel HBK gun](/patterns/parallel-hbk-gun).
