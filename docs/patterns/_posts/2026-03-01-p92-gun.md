description: |
  A glider gun with a [true](/patterns/true) period of 92.  The first one was
  found by Bill Gosper in 1971 using a period doubling reaction using
  two p46 guns.  Many different p92 guns are known that use multiple
  [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle)s.  A period 92 gun can also be made by adding a
  [semi-cenark](/patterns/semi-cenark) to any period 46 glider gun.
  On 18 November 2017, Martin Grant found a new gun using one twin
  bees shuttle and one [Tanner's p46](/patterns/tanner-s-p46) oscillator, making it the
  smallest known p92 gun.
