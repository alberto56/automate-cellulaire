description: |
  A perturbation moving within a stable pattern.  Dean
  Hickerson has written a [search program](/patterns/search-program) to search for drifters, with
  the hope of finding one which could be moved around a track.  Because
  drifters can be very small, they could be packed more tightly than
  [Herschel](/patterns/herschel)s, and so allow the creation of [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator)s of periods
  not yet attained, and possibly prove that Life is [omniperiodic](/patterns/omniperiodic).
  Hickerson has found a number of components towards this end, but it
  has proved difficult to change the direction of movement of a
  drifter, and so far no complete track has been found.  However,
  Hickerson has had success using the same program to find [eater](/patterns/eater)s
  with novel properties, such as [sparking eater](/patterns/sparking-eater)s and the ones shown
  in [diuresis](/patterns/diuresis).
