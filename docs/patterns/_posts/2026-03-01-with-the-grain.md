  A term used for [negative spaceship](/patterns/negative-spaceship)s travelling in
  [zebra stripes](/patterns/zebra-stripes) agar, parallel to the stripes, and also for
  [with-the-grain grey ship](/patterns/with-the-grain-grey-ship)s.
  Below are three small examples of "negative spaceships" found by
  Gabriel Nivasch in July 1999, travelling with the grain through a
  stabilized finite segment of zebra stripes agar.
