description: |
  A very rare type of [fuse](/patterns/fuse) whose output is identical
  to its input, possibly with some spatial and/or temporal offset.  See
  [lightspeed wire](/patterns/lightspeed-wire) for an example.  Reburnable fuses are used
  primarily in the construction of fixed-speed [self-supporting](/patterns/self-supporting)
  [macro-spaceship](/patterns/macro-spaceship)s, where the speed of the fuse's burning reaction
  becomes the speed of the spaceship.  Examples include the
  [Caterpillar](/patterns/caterpillar), [Centipede](/patterns/centipede), and [waterbear](/patterns/waterbear).
