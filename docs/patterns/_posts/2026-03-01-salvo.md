description: |
  A collection of spaceships, usually gliders, all travelling in
  the same direction.  Any valid glider construction [recipe](/patterns/recipe) can be
  partitioned into no more than four salvos.  Compare [flotilla](/patterns/flotilla).  In
  contrast with a [convoy](/patterns/convoy), the spaceships in a salvo are usually
  consumed by the reactions that they cause.  Simple examples include
  [block pusher](/patterns/block-pusher) and [block pull](/patterns/block-pull).
  Salvos may be [slow](/patterns/slow) or [synchronized](/patterns/synchronized).  The following partially
  [synchronized](/patterns/synchronized) three-glider salvo produces an [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs) from a block.
