description: |
  Of a [gun](/patterns/gun), emitting two streams of [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s (or
  [rake](/patterns/rake)s) every period.  For examples, see [B-52 bomber](/patterns/b-52-bomber),
  [Simkin glider gun](/patterns/simkin-glider-gun), and [p246 gun](/patterns/p246-gun).  In most cases, the two streams
  are alternately emitted 1/2 period apart.  It is also possible for
  the two streams to be emitted simultaneously, as in this
  double-barrelled glider gun by Bill Gosper.
