  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) travelling at one seventh of the
  [speed of light](/patterns/speed-of-light).  The first such spaceship to be discovered was the
  diagonally travelling [lobster](/patterns/lobster), found by Matthias Merzenich in
  August 2011.  The first known orthogonal c/7 spaceship was the
  [loafer](/patterns/loafer), discovered by Josh Ball in February 2013.  A
  [Caterloopillar](/patterns/caterloopillar) has been configured to move at c/7.
