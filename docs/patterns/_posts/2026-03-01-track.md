description: |
  A path made out of [conduit](/patterns/conduit)s, often ending where it begins so
  that the active [signal](/patterns/signal) object is cycled forever, forming an
  [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) or a [gun](/patterns/gun).
  This term has also been used to refer to the [lane](/patterns/lane) on which a
  [glider](/patterns/glider) or [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) travels.  The concept is very similar, but a
  reference to a "track" now usually implies a non-trivial supporting
  conduit.
