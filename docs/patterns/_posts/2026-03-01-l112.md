description: |
  A [composite conduit](/patterns/composite-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Dave Buckingham in July 1996.  It
  is made up of two [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit)s, HLx53B +  [BFx59H](/patterns/bfx59h).  After
  112 ticks, it produces a [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) turned 90 degrees
  counterclockwise at (12, -33) relative to the input.  Its
  [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) is 61 ticks; this can be reduced slightly by removing
  the output glider, either with a specialized eater (as in the
  original [true](/patterns/true) p59 gun), or with a [sparker](/patterns/sparker) as in most of the
  [Quetzal](/patterns/quetzal) guns.  It can be made [Spartan](/patterns/spartan) by replacing the
  [aircraft carrier](/patterns/aircraft-carrier) with an [eater1](/patterns/eater1).  A [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the
  pattern below marks the output location.
