description: |
  The following glider-supported [Herschel climber](/patterns/herschel-climber) reaction used in the [self-supporting](/patterns/self-supporting) [waterbear](/patterns/waterbear)[knightship](/patterns/knightship), which can be repeated every 79 ticks, moving the[Herschel](/patterns/herschel) 23 cells to the right and 5 cells upward, and releasing two [glider](/patterns/glider)s to the northwest and southwest.  As the diagram shows, it is possible to substitute a loaf or other [still life](/patterns/still-life)s for some or all of the support gliders.  This fact is used to advantage at the front end of the waterbear.
