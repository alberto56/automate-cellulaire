description: |
  (c/3 orthogonally, p3)  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) discovered by David
  Bell in early 1992, with a minimum of 25 cells - the lowest number of
  cells known for any c/3 spaceship.  A note in
  [Spaceships in Conway's Life](/patterns/spaceships-in-conway-s-life) indicates that it was found with a
  search that limited the number of live cells in each column, and
  possibly also the maximum cross-section (4 cells in this case).  See
  also [edge-repair spaceship](/patterns/edge-repair-spaceship) for a very similar c/3 spaceship with a
  minimum population of 26. In December 2017 a collaborative effort found a 26-glider synthesis
  for this spaceship.
