description: |
  A [glider gun](/patterns/glider-gun) with [true](/patterns/true) period 30.  The first one, found
  by Bill Gosper in November 1970 (see [Gosper glider gun](/patterns/gosper-glider-gun)), was also
  the first gun found of any period.  All known p30 glider guns are
  made from two or more interacting [queen bee shuttle](/patterns/queen-bee-shuttle)s. Paul Callahan
  found 30 different ways that three [queen bee shuttle](/patterns/queen-bee-shuttle)s can react to
  form a period 30 glider gun.  One of the most interesting of these is
  shown below in which the gliders emerge in an unexpected direction.
