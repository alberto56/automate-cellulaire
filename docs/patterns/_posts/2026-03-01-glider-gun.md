description: |
  A [gun](/patterns/gun) that fires [glider](/patterns/glider)s.  For examples, see
  [Gosper glider gun](/patterns/gosper-glider-gun), [Simkin glider gun](/patterns/simkin-glider-gun), [new gun](/patterns/new-gun),  [p45 gun](/patterns/p45-gun).
  True-period glider guns are known for some low periods, and for all
  periods over 53 using [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit) [technology](/patterns/technology).  See [true](/patterns/true)
  for a list of known true-period guns.  The lowest true-period gun
  possible is the [p14 gun](/patterns/p14-gun) since that is the lowest possible period
  for any glider [stream](/patterns/stream), but no example has yet been found.
  Pseudo-period glider guns are known for every period above 13.
  These are made by using multiple true-period guns of some multiple of
  the period, and glider [inject](/patterns/inject)ion methods to fill in the gaps.
