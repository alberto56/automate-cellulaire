description: |
  The smallest number of generations it takes for an [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator)
  or [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) to reappear in its original form, possibly subject to
  some rotation or reflection.  The mod may be equal to the [period](/patterns/period),
  but it may also be a quarter of the period (for oscillators that
  rotate 90 degrees every quarter period) or half the period (for other
  oscillators which rotate 180 degrees every half period, and also for
  [flipper](/patterns/flipper)s).
