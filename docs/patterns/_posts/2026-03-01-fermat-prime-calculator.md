description: |
  A pattern constructed by Jason Summers in
  January 2000 that exhibits [infinite growth](/patterns/infinite-growth) if and only if there are
  no Fermat primes greater than 65537.  The question of whether or not
  it really does exhibit infinite growth is therefore equivalent to a
  well-known and long-standing unsolved mathematical problem. It will,
  however, still be growing at generation 10^2585827975. The pattern is
  based on Dean Hickerson's [primer](/patterns/primer) and [caber tosser](/patterns/caber-tosser) patterns and a
  p8 [beehive](/patterns/beehive) [puffer](/patterns/puffer) by Hartmut Holzwart.
