description: |
  Any p2 [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator).  However, the term is also used in two
  more specific (and non-equivalent) senses: (a) any p2 oscillator
  whose two [phase](/patterns/phase)s are mirror images of one another, and (b) any p2
  oscillator in which all [rotor](/patterns/rotor) cells die from [underpopulation](/patterns/underpopulation).  In
  the latter sense it contrasts with [on-off](/patterns/on-off). The term has also been
  used even more specifically for the 12-cell flip-flop shown under
  [phoenix](/patterns/phoenix).
