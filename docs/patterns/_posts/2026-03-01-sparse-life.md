description: |
  This refers to the study of the evolution of a Life
  universe which starts off as a random [soup](/patterns/soup) of extremely low
  density.  Such a universe is dominated at an early stage by [block](/patterns/block)s
  and [blinker](/patterns/blinker)s (often referred to collectively as [blonk](/patterns/blonk)s) in a
  ratio of about 2:1.  Much later it will be dominated by simple
  [infinite growth](/patterns/infinite-growth) patterns (presumably mostly [switch engine](/patterns/switch-engine)s).  The
  long-term fate of a sparse Life universe is less certain.  It may
  possibly become dominated by self-reproducing patterns (see
  [universal constructor](/patterns/universal-constructor)), but it is not at all clear that there is
  any mechanism for these to deal with all the junk produced by switch
  engines.
