description: |
  A [composite conduit](/patterns/composite-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Dave Buckingham in July 1996.  It
  is made up of two [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit)s, [HFx58B](/patterns/hfx58b) + [BFx59H](/patterns/bfx59h).  After
  117 ticks, it produces a [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) at (40, -6) relative to the
  input.  Its [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) is 63 ticks.  It can be made [Spartan](/patterns/spartan) by
  replacing the [snake](/patterns/snake) with an [eater1](/patterns/eater1) in one of two orientations.  A
  [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern below marks the output location.
