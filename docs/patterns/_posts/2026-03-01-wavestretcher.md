description: |
  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) pattern that supports a connection to an
  extensible periodic [wick](/patterns/wick)-like structure, whose speed and/or
  direction of propagation are different from those of the
  wavestretcher spaceship.
  Connecting the following to a standard diagonal [antstretcher](/patterns/antstretcher)
  creates a new oblique [wavestretcher](/patterns/wavestretcher) (a type of [growing spaceship](/patterns/growing-spaceship))
  and also an alternate [space nonfiller](/patterns/space-nonfiller) mechanism.
