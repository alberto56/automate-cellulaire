description: |
  Any [wickstretcher](/patterns/wickstretcher) in which the wick is two diagonal
  lines of cells forming, successively, a [tub](/patterns/tub), a [barge](/patterns/barge), a
  [long barge](/patterns/long-barge), etc.  The first one was found by Hartmut Holzwart in
  June 1993, although at the time this was considered to be a
  boatstretcher (as it was shown with an extra cell, making the tub
  into a [boat](/patterns/boat)).  The following small example is by Nicolay Beluchenko
  (August 2005), using a [quarter](/patterns/quarter).
