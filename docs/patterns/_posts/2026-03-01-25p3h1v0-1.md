  (c/3 orthogonally, p3)  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) discovered by Dean
  Hickerson in August 1989.  It was the first c/3 spaceship to be
  discovered. In terms of its 25 cells, it is tied with [25P3H1V0.2](/patterns/25p3h1v0.2) as
  the smallest c/3 spaceship.  Unlike 25P3H1V0.2, it has a population
  of 25 in all of its phases, as well as a smaller bounding box. Martin Grant discovered a glider synthesis for 25P3H1V0.1 on 6
  January 2015.
