description: |
  To change the fate of an object by reacting it with other
  objects.  Typically, the other objects are sparks from [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s
  or [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator)s, or are [eater](/patterns/eater)s or impacting spaceships.
  Perturbations are typically done to turn a [dirty](/patterns/dirty) reaction into a
  [clean](/patterns/clean) one, or to change the products of a reaction. In many
  desirable cases the perturbing objects are not destroyed by the
  reaction, or else are easily replenished.
