description: |
  (c/2, p36)  Generally, any [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)
  constructed using [pufferfish](/patterns/pufferfish).  May refer specifically to the
  extensible c/2 [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) constructed by Ivan Fomichev in December
  2014, the first such spaceship to contain no period-2 or period-4
  parts.  (The first two or three rows might be considered to be period
  2 or 4, but they are directly dependent on following rows for
  support.).
  The pattern consists of two adjacent [pufferfish](/patterns/pufferfish) [puffer](/patterns/puffer)s, plus
  four copies of a nontrivial period 36 c/2 [fuse](/patterns/fuse) for pufferfish
  [exhaust](/patterns/exhaust), discovered using a randomized soup search.
