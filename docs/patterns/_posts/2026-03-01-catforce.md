description: |
  An optimized [search program](/patterns/search-program) written by Michael Simkin in
  2015, using brute-force enumeration of small [Spartan](/patterns/spartan) objects in a
  limited area, instead of a depth-first tree search.  One major
  purpose of CatForce is to find glider-constructible completions for
  signal conduits.  An early CatForce discovery was the [B60](/patterns/b60) conduit,
  which enabled a record-breaking new glider gun.
