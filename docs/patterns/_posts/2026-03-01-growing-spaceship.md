description: |
  An object that moves like a [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship), except
  that its front part moves faster than its back part and a [wick](/patterns/wick)
  extends between the two.  Put another way, a growing spaceship is a
  [puffer](/patterns/puffer) whose output is burning [clean](/patterns/clean)ly at a slower rate than the
  puffer is producing it.  Examples include [blinker ship](/patterns/blinker-ship)s,
  [pi ship](/patterns/pi-ship)s, and some [wavestretcher](/patterns/wavestretcher)s.
