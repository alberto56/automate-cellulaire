description: |
  (p1)  A compound [eater](/patterns/eater) that can eat [glider](/patterns/glider)s coming from two
  different directions. Also called the tub-with-tail eater (TWIT), it
  is often placed along the edges of glider [lane](/patterns/lane)s to suppress
  unwanted gliders in [conduit](/patterns/conduit)s. Below is the standard form, a compact
  form with a [long hook](/patterns/long-hook), and an often-useful conjoined form found
  with [Bellman](/patterns/bellman).  The [sidesnagger](/patterns/sidesnagger) is a Spartan constellation that
  has a similar glider-absorbing function, using a [loaf](/patterns/loaf).  See also
  [7x9 eater](/patterns/7x9-eater).
