description: |
  A [growing spaceship](/patterns/growing-spaceship) in which the wick consists of a
  line of [blinker](/patterns/blinker)s.  An example by Paul Schick based on his
  [Schick engine](/patterns/schick-engine) is shown below.  Here the front part is p12 and moves
  at c/2, while the back part is p26 and moves at 6c/13.  Every 156
  generations 13 blinkers are created and 12 are destroyed, so the wick
  becomes one blinker longer.
