description: |
  A small stable 90-degree glider reflector with a repeat time of
  43 ticks, discovered by Mike Playle on 25 April 2013 using a search
  utility he wrote called [Bellman](/patterns/bellman). Compare [boojum reflector](/patterns/boojum-reflector).  Four
  common Snark variants are shown below:  Playle's original at the top,
  and variants by Heinrich Koenig, Simon Ekstrom, and Shannon Omick to
  the left, bottom, and right, respectively.  As of June 2018, only
  Playle's variant has a known [slow glider construction](/patterns/slow-glider-construction) [recipe](/patterns/recipe) for
  all orientations.
