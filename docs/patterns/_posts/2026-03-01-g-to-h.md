description: |
  A [converter](/patterns/converter) that takes a [glider](/patterns/glider) as an input [signal](/patterns/signal) and
  produces a [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) output, which can then be used by other
  [conduit](/patterns/conduit)s.  G-to-Hs are frequently used in [stable](/patterns/stable) logic circuitry.
  Early examples include [Callahan G-to-H](/patterns/callahan-g-to-h), [Silver G-to-H](/patterns/silver-g-to-h), and
  [p8 G-to-H](/patterns/p8-g-to-h) for periodic circuits.  A more compact recent example is
  the [syringe](/patterns/syringe).
