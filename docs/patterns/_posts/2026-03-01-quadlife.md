description: |
  A form of [colourised Life](/patterns/colourised-life) in which there are four types of
  ON cell.  A newly-born cell takes the type of the majority of its
  three [parent cells](/patterns/parent-cells), or the remaining type if its parent cells are
  all of different types.  In areas where there are only two types of
  ON cell QuadLife reduces to [Immigration](/patterns/immigration).
