description: |
  A [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship) travelling at one fifth of the
  [speed of light](/patterns/speed-of-light).  The first such spaceship to be discovered was the
  [snail](/patterns/snail), found by Tim Coe in January 1996.  The first diagonally
  moving example, [295P5H1V1](/patterns/295p5h1v1), was found by Jason Summers in November
  2000.  For other c/5 ships see [58P5H1V1](/patterns/58p5h1v1), [67P5H1V1](/patterns/67p5h1v1), [86P5H1V1](/patterns/86p5h1v1) and
  [spider](/patterns/spider).  A [Caterloopillar](/patterns/caterloopillar) has also been configured to move at
  c/5.
