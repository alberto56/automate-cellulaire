description: |
  Even or odd, particularly as applied to the [phase](/patterns/phase) of an
  oscillator or spaceship.  For example, in [slow salvo](/patterns/slow-salvo) constructions,
  the [intermediate target](/patterns/intermediate-target)s are frequently period 2, most often
  because they contain [blinker](/patterns/blinker)s or [traffic light](/patterns/traffic-light)s.  A glider
  striking a P2 constellation will generally produce a different result
  depending on its parity. Period-4 intermediate targets are rare (or
  not used), so it doesn't matter for example whether an odd-parity
  glider in a slow salvo is phase 1 or phase 3.  Only the even/odd
  parity is important.
