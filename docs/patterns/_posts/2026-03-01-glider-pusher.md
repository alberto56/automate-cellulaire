description: |
  An arrangement of a [queen bee shuttle](/patterns/queen-bee-shuttle) and a
  [pentadecathlon](/patterns/pentadecathlon) that can push the path of a passing glider out by
  one half-diagonal space.  This was found by Dieter Leithner in
  December 1993 and is shown below.  It is useful for constructing
  complex [gun](/patterns/gun)s where it may be necessary to produce a number of
  gliders travelling on close parallel paths.  See also [edge shooter](/patterns/edge-shooter).
