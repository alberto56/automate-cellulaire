description: |
  Either of two [semi-Snark](/patterns/semi-snark) variants discovered by Tanner
  Jacobi in November 2017.  The name is due to the initial [converter](/patterns/converter),
  which produces a [century](/patterns/century) output for every two input [glider](/patterns/glider)s.  The
  minimum safe repeat time is 43 ticks for the smaller initial
  [catalyst](/patterns/catalyst) shown in [CC semi-cenark](/patterns/cc-semi-cenark) and [CP semi-cenark](/patterns/cp-semi-cenark), or 42
  ticks with the slightly larger catalyst variant shown below.  There
  is also [overclocking](/patterns/overclocking) possible at period 36, 38, or 39.  The reset
  glider can be followed immediately by a new trigger glider, as shown
  below, so the minimum repeat time for an [intermittent stream](/patterns/intermittent-stream) of
  gliders is only 50 ticks.
