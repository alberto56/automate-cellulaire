description: |
  (c/2 orthogonally, p12)  This [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship), found by Paul
  Schick in 1972, produces a large [spark](/patterns/spark) (the 15 live cells at the
  rear in the [phase](/patterns/phase) shown below) which can be [perturb](/patterns/perturb)ed by other
  c/2 spaceships to form a variety of [puffer](/patterns/puffer)s.  See [blinker ship](/patterns/blinker-ship)
  for an example perturbation of the spark.  The diagram below shows
  the smallest form of the Schick engine, using two [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs).  It is also
  possible to use two [MWSS](/patterns/mwSs)es or two [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs)es, or even an LWSS and an
  HWSS.
