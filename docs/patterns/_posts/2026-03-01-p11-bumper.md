description: |
  (p11)  A periodic [colour-preserving](/patterns/colour-preserving) [glider](/patterns/glider) [reflector](/patterns/reflector)
  with a minimum [repeat time](/patterns/repeat-time) of 44 ticks.  Unlike the p5 through p8
  cases where Noam Elkies' [domino](/patterns/domino)-spark based reflectors are
  available, no small period-22 [colour-changing](/patterns/colour-changing) reflector is known.
  A [stable](/patterns/stable) [Snark](/patterns/snark) reflector can be substituted for any [bumper](/patterns/bumper).
  This changes the timing of the output glider, which can be useful for
  rephasing periodic glider streams.
