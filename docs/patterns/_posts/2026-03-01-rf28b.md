description: |
  A [converter](/patterns/converter) with several known forms, many of which found by
  Dave Buckingham in 1972 and in the early 1980s.  It accepts an
  [R-pentomino](/patterns/r-pentomino) as input and produces an output [B-heptomino](/patterns/b-heptomino) 28 ticks
  later.  Of nine major variants known as of July 2018, four versions
  are shown below.  For each version, the R-pentomino inputs are shown
  near the left and right edges, along with the B-heptomino output
  locations near the center.      The version in the southeast is used in Paul Callahan's
  [Herschel receiver](/patterns/herschel-receiver).  The one in the northwest is part of [L156](/patterns/l156), but
  can be replaced by the variant in the northeast which produces a
  forward glider output.
