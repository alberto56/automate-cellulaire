description: |
  (c/2 orthogonally, p4)  A lightweight spaceship, the smallest
  known orthogonally moving [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship), and the second most common
  (after the [glider](/patterns/glider)).  Found by Conway when one formed from a random
  soup in 1970.  See also [MWSS](/patterns/mwSs) and [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs).

    The LWSS possesses a [tail spark](/patterns/tail-spark) which can easily [perturb](/patterns/perturb) other

    some objects in additional ways.  For examples, see [blinker ship](/patterns/blinker-ship),
    [hivenudger](/patterns/hivenudger), and [puffer train](/patterns/puffer-train).
    Dave Buckingham found that the LWSS can be synthesized in several
    different ways using three gliders, and can be constructed from two
    gliders and another small object in several more ways.  Here is the
