description: |
  A dying [spark](/patterns/spark) made by removing one cell from the
  [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) heptomino.  This particular spark has the advantage that,
  when placed in a conduit to mark the location of an input or output
  Herschel, it disappears cleanly without damaging adjacent catalysts,
  even in [dependent conduit](/patterns/dependent-conduit)s with a block only two cells away.
