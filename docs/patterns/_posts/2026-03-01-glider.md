description: |
  (c/4 diagonally, p4)  The smallest, most common and first
  discovered [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship).  This was found by Richard Guy in 1970 while
  Conway's group was attempting to track the [evolution](/patterns/evolution) of the
  [R-pentomino](/patterns/r-pentomino).  The name is due in part to the fact that it is
  [glide symmetric](/patterns/glide-symmetric).  (It is often stated that Conway discovered the
  glider, but he himself has said it was Guy.  See also the cryptic
  reference ("some guy") in [Winning Ways](/patterns/winning-ways).)
