description: |
  (p15)  Found in 1970 by Conway while tracking the
  history of short rows of cells, 10 cells giving this object, which is
  the most [natural](/patterns/natural) [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) of period greater than 3.  In fact it
  is the fifth most common [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) overall, appearing in random
  soups slightly more frequently than the [clock](/patterns/clock), but much less
  frequently than the [blinker](/patterns/blinker), [toad](/patterns/toad), [beacon](/patterns/beacon) or [pulsar](/patterns/pulsar).  The
  pentadecathlon can be constructed using just three gliders, as shown
  in [glider synthesis](/patterns/glider-synthesis).

    [V spark](/patterns/v-spark)s and [domino](/patterns/domino) sparks, which give it a great capacity for
    doing [perturbation](/patterns/perturbation)s, especially for period 30 based [technology](/patterns/technology).
