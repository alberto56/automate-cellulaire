description: |
  Opposite of [clean](/patterns/clean).  A reaction which produces a large amount
  of complicated junk which is difficult to control or use is said to
  be dirty.  Many basic [puffer engine](/patterns/puffer-engine)s are dirty and need to be
  [tame](/patterns/tame)d by accompanying [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s in order to produce clean
  output.  Similarly, a dirty [conduit](/patterns/conduit) is one that does not recover
  perfectly after the passage of a [signal](/patterns/signal); one or more extra [ash](/patterns/ash)
  objects are left behind (or more rarely a [catalyst](/patterns/catalyst) is damaged) and
  additional signals must be used to clean up the circuit before it can
  be re-used.
