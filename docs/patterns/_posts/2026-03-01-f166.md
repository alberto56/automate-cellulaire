description: |
  A [composite conduit](/patterns/composite-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Paul Callahan in May 1997.  It is
  composed of two [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit)s, HFx107B + [BFx59H](/patterns/bfx59h).  The F166
  and [Lx200](/patterns/lx200) conduits are the two original [dependent conduit](/patterns/dependent-conduit)s
  (several more have since been discovered).  After 166 ticks, it
  produces a [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) at (49, 3) relative to the input.  Its
  [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) is 116 ticks.  A [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern
  below marks the output location.
