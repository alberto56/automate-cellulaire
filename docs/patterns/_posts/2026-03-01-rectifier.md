description: |
  The smallest known 180-degree [reflector](/patterns/reflector), discovered by
  Adam P. Goucher in 2009.  It was the smallest and fastest stable
  reflector of any kind until the discovery of the [Snark](/patterns/snark) in 2013. The
  rectifier has the same output glider as the [boojum reflector](/patterns/boojum-reflector) but a
  much shorter [repeat time](/patterns/repeat-time) of only 106 ticks.
  Another advantage of the rectifier is that the output glider is on
  a [transparent lane](/patterns/transparent-lane), so it can be used in logic circuitry to merge
  two signal paths.
