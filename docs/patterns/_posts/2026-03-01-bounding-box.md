description: |
  The smallest rectangular array of cells that contains
  the whole of a given pattern.  For [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator)s and [gun](/patterns/gun)s this
  usually is meant to include all [phase](/patterns/phase)s of the pattern, but in the
  case of guns, the outgoing stream(s) are excluded.  The bounding box
  is one of the standard ways to measure the size of an object; the
  other standard metric is the [population](/patterns/population).
