description: |
  Period 1, i.e., [stable](/patterns/stable).  In the context of logic [circuit](/patterns/circuit)ry,
  this tends to mean that a mechanism is constructed from
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s that contain only [still life](/patterns/still-life)s as [catalyst](/patterns/catalyst)s.
  In the context of [slow glider construction](/patterns/slow-glider-construction), a P1 [slow salvo](/patterns/slow-salvo) is
  one in which there are no constraints on the [parity](/patterns/parity) of gliders in
  the salvo, because the [intermediate target](/patterns/intermediate-target)s are all stable
  constellations.  (The usual alternative is a "P2 slow salvo", where
  the relative timing between adjacent gliders can be increased
  arbitrarily, but only by multiples of two ticks.)
