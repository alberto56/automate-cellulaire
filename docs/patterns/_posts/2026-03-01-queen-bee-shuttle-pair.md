description: |
  Any arrangement of two [queen bee shuttle](/patterns/queen-bee-shuttle)s
  such that the two [beehive](/patterns/beehive)s created between them are consumed in
  some way. There are many ways that the two shuttles can be placed,
  either head-to-head, or else at right angles.  The most well-known
  and useful arrangement results in the [Gosper glider gun](/patterns/gosper-glider-gun).
  Other arrangements don't create any lasting output, but create
  large [spark](/patterns/spark)s which can perturb objects (especially gliders) in
  various ways.  For example, one arrangement of a queen bee shuttle
  pair was used in the original [unit Life cell](/patterns/unit-life-cell) as a [memory cell](/patterns/memory-cell).
  Here an input glider is converted into a block, which remains until
  it is deleted by a glider on a right-angled path.
