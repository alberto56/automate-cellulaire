description: |
  An object that participates in a reaction but emerges from
  it unharmed.  All [eater](/patterns/eater)s are catalysts.  Some small [still life](/patterns/still-life)s
  can act as catalysts in some situations, such as the [block](/patterns/block), [ship](/patterns/ship),
  and [tub](/patterns/tub).  The still lifes and oscillators that form a [conduit](/patterns/conduit) are
  examples of catalysts.
  A relatively rare form of catalysis occurs in a
  [transparent debris effect](/patterns/transparent-debris-effect), where the catalyst in question is
  completely destroyed and then rebuilt.  The term is also sometimes
  used for a modification of an active reaction in a [rake](/patterns/rake) by passing
  [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s.
