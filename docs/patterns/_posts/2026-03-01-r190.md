description: |
  A [composite conduit](/patterns/composite-conduit), one of the original sixteen
  [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s, discovered by Dave Buckingham in July 1996.  It
  is made up of two [elementary conduit](/patterns/elementary-conduit)s, HRx131B + [BFx59H](/patterns/bfx59h).  After
  190 ticks, it produces a [Herschel](/patterns/herschel) turned 90 degrees clockwise at
  (24, 16) relative to the input.  Its [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) is 107 ticks.  A
  [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) in the pattern below marks the output location.
