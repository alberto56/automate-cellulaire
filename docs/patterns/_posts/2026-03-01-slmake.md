description: |
  A [search program](/patterns/search-program) published by Adam P. Goucher in May 2017.
  It accepts as input a [constellation](/patterns/constellation) of sufficiently widely
  separated [still life](/patterns/still-life)s, and produces a [glider](/patterns/glider) [stream](/patterns/stream) that will
  perform a complete [slow glider construction](/patterns/slow-glider-construction) of that constellation,
  starting from a single block.
  One of slmake's primary uses is to make [self-constructing](/patterns/self-constructing)
  patterns much easier to design and build.  It is capable of finding
  [recipe](/patterns/recipe)s not only for [Spartan](/patterns/spartan) [stable](/patterns/stable) [circuit](/patterns/circuit)ry, but also for
  other useful non-Spartan circuits such as [Snark](/patterns/snark)s, [syringe](/patterns/syringe)s, and
  [H-to-MWSS](/patterns/h-to-mwSs) [converter](/patterns/converter)s, provided that they are separated from other
  nearby objects by a sufficient amount of empty space.
