description: |
  A [glider gun](/patterns/glider-gun) with a [true](/patterns/true) period of 44.  The first one was
  found by Dave Buckingham in April 1992.  It uses two interacting
  copies of an [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) which he also found.  In 1996 he found a
  gun which only used one copy of the oscillator.  Paul Callahan
  improved it in 1997, resulting in the gun shown below.
