description: |
  A glider injection gate.  This is a device for [inject](/patterns/inject)ing a
  [glider](/patterns/glider) into a glider [stream](/patterns/stream).  The injected glider is synthesized
  from one or more incoming [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s assisted by the presence of
  the GIG.  (This contrasts with some other glider injection reactions
  which do not require a GIG,  as in [inject](/patterns/inject).)  Gliders already in the
  glider stream pass through the GIG without interfering with it.  A
  GIG usually consists of a small number of oscillators.
  For example, in July 1996 Dieter Leithner found the following
  reaction which allows the construction of a pseudo-period 14 glider
  stream.  It uses two [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs) streams, a [pentadecathlon](/patterns/pentadecathlon) and a
  [volcano](/patterns/volcano).
