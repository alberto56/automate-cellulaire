description: |
  A regular growth that is sometimes formed when a stream of
  [glider](/patterns/glider)s, or other [spaceship](/patterns/spaceship)s, is fired into some junk.
  The most common example is initiated by the following collision of
  a glider with a [block](/patterns/block).  With a glider stream of even [period](/patterns/period) at
  least 82, this gives a crystal which forms a pair of [beehive](/patterns/beehive)s for
  every 11 gliders which hit it.
