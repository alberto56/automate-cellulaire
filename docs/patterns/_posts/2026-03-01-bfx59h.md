description: |
  One of the earliest and most remarkable [converter](/patterns/converter)s,
  discovered by Dave Buckingham in July 1996.  In 59 generations it
  transforms a B-heptomino into a clean Herschel with very good
  clearance, allowing easy connections to other conduits.  It forms the
  final stage of many of the known [composite conduit](/patterns/composite-conduit)s, including the
  majority of the original sixteen [Herschel conduit](/patterns/herschel-conduit)s.  Here a
  [ghost Herschel](/patterns/ghost-herschel) marks the output location.
