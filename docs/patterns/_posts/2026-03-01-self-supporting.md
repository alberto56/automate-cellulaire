description: |
  A type of pattern, specifically a [macro-spaceship](/patterns/macro-spaceship),
  that constructs [signal](/patterns/signal)s or [track](/patterns/track)s or other scaffolding to assist
  its movement, but does not contain complete information about its own
  structure.  Examples include the Caterpillar, [Centipede](/patterns/centipede),
  [half-baked knightship](/patterns/half-baked-knightship), [waterbear](/patterns/waterbear), and the [Caterloopillar](/patterns/caterloopillar)s.
  [Caterpillar](/patterns/caterpillar) has been used as a general term for self-supporting
  spaceships, but it is not very appropriate for the HBKs.
  In general a self-supporting pattern cannot be trivially adjusted
  to alter its speed or direction.  The variable speeds of the HBKs and
  the Caterloopillars are exceptions, but their direction of travel is
  fixed, and a specific Caterloopillar can't be made to change its
  speed without completely rebuilding it.  Compare [self-constructing](/patterns/self-constructing),
  [elementary](/patterns/elementary).
