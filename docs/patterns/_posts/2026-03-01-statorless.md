description: |
  A statorless [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) is one in which no cell is
  permanently on - that is, the [stator](/patterns/stator) is empty,  or in other words
  the oscillator has the maximum possible volatility.  See the
  [volatility](/patterns/volatility) entry for examples of this type of oscillator at
  different periods.  Statorless oscillators can be constructed for any
  sufficiently large period, using [universal constructor](/patterns/universal-constructor) technology.
