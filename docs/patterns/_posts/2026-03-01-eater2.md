  (p1)  This [eater](/patterns/eater) was found by Dave Buckingham in the 1970s.
  Mostly it works like the ordinary [eater1](/patterns/eater1) but with two slight
  differences that make it useful despite its size: it takes longer to
  recover from each bite, and it can eat objects appearing at two
  different positions.
