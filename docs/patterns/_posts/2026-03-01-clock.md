  [beacon](/patterns/beacon) or [pulsar](/patterns/pulsar).  It is surprisingly rare considering its small
  size.

  description: |
    The protruding cells at the edges can perturb some reactions by
    inhibiting the birth of a cell in a 3-cell corner.  For example, a
    clock can be used to suppress the surplus [blinker](/patterns/blinker) produced by an
    [F171](/patterns/f171) conduit, significantly improving the [recovery time](/patterns/recovery-time) of the
    circuit.
