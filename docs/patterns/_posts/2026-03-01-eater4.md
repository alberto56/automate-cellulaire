  (p1)  Another [eater](/patterns/eater) by Dave Buckingham, which he found in
  1971, but did not recognize as an eater until 1975 or 1976.  It can't
  eat [glider](/patterns/glider)s, but it can be used for various other purposes. The
  four NE-most centre cells regrow in a few generations after being
  destroyed by taking a bite out of something, such as suppressing half
  of a developing [traffic light](/patterns/traffic-light) as it does in the
  [p29 pentadecathlon hassler](/patterns/p29-pentadecathlon-hassler).
