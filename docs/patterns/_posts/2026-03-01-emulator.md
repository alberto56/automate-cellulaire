description: |
  Any one of three p4 oscillators that produce [spark](/patterns/spark)s
  similar to those produced by [LWSS](/patterns/lwSs), [MWSS](/patterns/mwSs) and [HWSS](/patterns/hwSs).  See
  [LW emulator](/patterns/lw-emulator), [MW emulator](/patterns/mw-emulator) and [HW emulator](/patterns/hw-emulator).  Larger emulators are
  also possible, but they require stabilizing objects to suppress their
  [non-spark](/patterns/non-spark)s and so are of little use.  The emulators were discovered
  by Robert Wainwright in June 1980.
