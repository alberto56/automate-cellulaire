  Any [oscillator](/patterns/oscillator) which consists of an active region moving
  back and forth between stabilizing objects.  The most well-known
  examples are the [queen bee shuttle](/patterns/queen-bee-shuttle) (which has often been called
  simply "the shuttle") and the [twin bees shuttle](/patterns/twin-bees-shuttle).  See also
  [p54 shuttle](/patterns/p54-shuttle), [p130 shuttle](/patterns/p130-shuttle) and [Eureka](/patterns/eureka).  Another example is the
  p72 [R-pentomino](/patterns/r-pentomino) shuttle that forms part of the pattern given under
  [factory](/patterns/factory).
