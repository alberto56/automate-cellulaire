description: |
  A [Spartan](/patterns/spartan) logic circuit discovered by Tanner
  Jacobi on 12 May 2015.  It converts an input [glider](/patterns/glider) [signal](/patterns/signal) into a
  [beehive](/patterns/beehive), in such a way that the beehive can cleanly absorb a single
  glider from a perpendicular glider [stream](/patterns/stream).  The circuit can't be
  re-used until the beehive "bit" is cleared by the passage of at least
  one perpendicular input. This term has sometimes been used for the beehive [catalyst](/patterns/catalyst)
  variant of [SW-2](/patterns/sw-2), and also for Paul Callahan's larger
  [glider stopper](/patterns/glider-stopper), which also provides optional 0-degree and
  180-degree glider outputs.
